Universal Setup Guide

1. Prepare Your Sheet: Create a Google Sheet. Ensure Row 1 contains your field names (headers).

2. Critical Sharing Step: Click the "Share" button. Change access to "Anyone with the link" and set the role to "Editor". The app will fail if this is not set to Editor.

3. Sync: Paste the URL in 'Connect File' and click Initialize. Your forms will generate instantly.

4. GPS Mapping: If your sheet has headers like 'Latitude' or 'Longitude', the app auto-populates your coordinates.
